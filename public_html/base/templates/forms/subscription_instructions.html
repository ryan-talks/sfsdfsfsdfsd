Individuals in temporary non-immigrant visa status have visas which begin with letters: B, F, J, TN, H, L, O, etc. On the other hand, an individual that has been granted a green card to live and work in the United States indefinitely is considered a lawful permanent resident under the immigration laws. Subscribers may access the IRS website (www.IRS.gov) to obtain the appropriate forms and instructions. Foreign Investors should consult their own personal tax advisors with regard to the effect of the United States federal, state and local tax laws and foreign laws on an investment in the Company, the completion of an Internal Revenue Service form and the potential that the Fund will be required to withhold U.S. federal, state and local income taxes from amounts otherwise distributable to foreign investors.
